			Arts and Crafts workshops allows seniors to practise their hand eye coordination skills. Hand eye coordination skills are important in many daily activities.  Art and Craft Workshops also brings colours to the seniorsâ€™ lives. It keeps them engaged and dispel feelings of loneliness which seniors might feel.<br>
